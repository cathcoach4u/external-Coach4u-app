★ Step 1 — Identify

Name your 3–6 core values

✓ Complete

Core values are the non-negotiables — the beliefs that guide how your company operates, regardless of who’s watching.

Tip: 3–5 values is ideal. Fewer is better — too many means none of them are truly non-negotiable.

✎ Step 2 — Define

What does each value mean to your company?

✓ Complete

Write a short, specific definition for each value in your own words — not dictionary definitions, but what it means here.

Tip: A good definition is 1–2 sentences that a new team member would immediately understand.

◎ Step 3 — Values in Action

What do your values look like in daily work?

✓ Complete

For each value, describe the behaviours you’d expect to see. This makes values observable and actionable — not just words on a wall.

Tip: Think about how you’d explain each value to a new team member on their first day. What would you point to?
