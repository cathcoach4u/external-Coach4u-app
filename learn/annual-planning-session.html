🗺️ Annual Planning Session

Two days, once a year, to refresh the entire One-Page Plan and set the year ahead.

What this is

The biggest session of the year. The leadership team refreshes values, focus, targets, marketing, financials, team — and commits to a 1-Year Plan.

Why it matters

The world changes more in 12 months than you think. An annual reset keeps your strategy from quietly drifting into irrelevance.

How to do it well

  1. Day 1 — Where are we? Numbers review (current year vs plan). Wins, losses, learnings. Surface every issue. Refresh values, focus, marketing.
  2. Day 1 evening: team dinner. Real conversations happen here.
  3. Day 2 — Where are we going? Refresh 10-year vision and 3-year outlook. Set 1-year plan + financials. Set Q1 priorities. Assign owners.
  4. End with a written One-Page Plan everyone has signed off on.

Common pitfalls

  • Running it in the office. Get offsite — physical removal helps strategic thinking.
  • Inviting too many people. Leadership team only. Bigger groups produce vaguer plans.
Coach’s tip
Run the Team Check-in 1–2 weeks beforehand. Read every response before Day 1. The session goes faster and lands harder when you arrive with the data.
