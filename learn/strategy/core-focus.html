🧭 Core Focus

Why your company exists and what you do better than anyone else. This is the anchor for every strategic decision.

The clearest core focus is brutally simple. Your purpose is the “why” that wouldn’t change even if your products did. Your niche is the one thing you do better than anyone else — not three, not five. When in doubt, narrower beats broader.
