📣 Marketing Strategy

Who you serve, what makes you different, the process clients walk through, and the guarantee that backs it.

What this is

Four short statements that together define how you go to market: target market, differentiators, your process, and your guarantee.

Why it matters

Without a clear position, every customer takes a slightly different journey, every salesperson tells a slightly different story, and your conversion rate suffers.

How to do it well

  1. Target market: be specific. Revenue range, team size, geography, the stage they're in.
  2. What makes us different: three things that are true and provable about you that aren't true of competitors. Avoid "we care more" — everyone says that.
  3. Our Process: name your delivery method. Make it a sequence the prospect can picture themselves walking through.
  4. Guarantee: what promise takes risk off the prospect? It doesn't have to be money-back — it can be a behavioural guarantee ("We'll respond within X").

Common pitfalls

  • Mistaking adjectives ("trusted, friendly") for differentiators. They're commodities.
  • A process that's just stages of your sale ("Discovery → Proposal → Close"). The client doesn't care about your funnel — name their journey.
Coach’s tip
Once defined, audit your website. Every key page should reflect this position. If your homepage and your About page tell different stories, prospects don't trust either.
